# Required SEO Packet Review — Validation Criteria

Validate whether the candidate result is a useful review pass over a supplied website analysis before sales-packet creation.

Pass only if all are true:
1. It references the target website or supplied analysis.
2. It includes a clear decision: `ready`, `needs revision`, or `blocked`.
3. It checks whether claims are supported by inspected-page evidence or marked `Not verified`.
4. It calls out missing evidence or says no major missing evidence was found.
5. It calls out weak/speculative claims or says no major speculative claims were found.
6. It checks local SEO, conversion, trust/proof, content quality, and AI/LLM readiness.
7. It provides recommended fixes or explains why none are needed.
8. It identifies an improved sales angle or explains why one cannot be determined.
9. It avoids unsupported claims about ratings, rankings, ownership, licensing, years in business, or response times.
10. It is specific enough to guide the next packet step.

Fail if it merely praises the analysis, omits the decision, lacks missing-evidence/speculation checks, or is generic.
