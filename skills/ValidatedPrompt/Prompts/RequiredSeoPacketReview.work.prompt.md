# Required SEO Packet Review — Work Prompt

Review the supplied website analysis before client-facing packet materials are created.

Inputs: website URL, website analysis/audit text, and any sales context. If the analysis is missing, say the review cannot be completed and list what is needed. Do not invent evidence.

Required work:
1. Check whether the analysis is evidence-backed and cites inspected pages or marks unknowns `Not verified`.
2. Check local SEO, service keyword coverage, crawlability, trust/social proof, conversion path, content quality, and AI/LLM readiness.
3. Identify unsupported or speculative claims.
4. Identify missing evidence needed before client-facing use.
5. Rewrite weak/generic recommendations into practical sales-useful recommendations.
6. Identify the strongest improved sales angle.
7. Decide whether the analysis is ready to feed into client-facing materials.

Output headings:
1. Review decision: `ready`, `needs revision`, or `blocked`
2. Evidence support check
3. Missing evidence
4. Weak or speculative claims
5. Recommended fixes before sales material
6. Improved sales angle
7. Required next action
