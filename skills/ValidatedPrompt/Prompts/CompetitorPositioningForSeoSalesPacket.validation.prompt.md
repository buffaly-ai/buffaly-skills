# Competitor Positioning For SEO Sales Packet — Validation Criteria

Validate whether the candidate result is a usable competitor/positioning section.

Pass only if all are true:
1. It references the target website, business, service category, or location.
2. It identifies actual business competitors or clearly explains why they could not be verified.
3. It separates actual businesses from directories, marketplaces, lead-generation sites, or SEO-only search competitors when relevant.
4. Competitor claims are tied to evidence, direct URLs, or marked `Not verified`.
5. It compares service coverage, local positioning, trust/proof, CTAs, or SEO/page structure.
6. It explains prospect positioning gaps.
7. It explains where the prospect can win in sales-useful language.
8. It avoids unsupported claims about rankings, ratings, years in business, licenses, or competitor performance.
9. It includes packet-ready talking points.
10. It is not generic competitor advice disconnected from the supplied website.

Fail if it invents competitors, fails to separate competitor types, omits evidence limits, or lacks sales-useful positioning.
