The second growth lever

Remote care: a revenue line worth validating

Your public site highlights immunizations, testing, med sync, delivery, and compounding. It does not visibly confirm these adjacent Medicare programs:

Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)

Bluetooth BP cuffs, scales, and glucometers transmit vitals to your team. Billed under CPT 99453 / 99454 / 99457.

Chronic Care Management (CCM)

Monthly care coordination for patients with 2+ chronic conditions. Billed under CPT 99490.

Annual Wellness Visits (AWV)

Health-risk assessments for Medicare beneficiaries. Billed under G0438 / G0439.
