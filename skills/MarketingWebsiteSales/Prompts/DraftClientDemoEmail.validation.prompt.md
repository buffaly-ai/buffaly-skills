# Validate Client Demo Email Draft

Validate that the work result drafted a prospect/client email for a website audit or demo follow-up.

## Objective checks

Fail if any required part is missing:

- Identifies recipient/business context and purpose of the email.
- Includes a subject line or subject options.
- Includes a complete email body.
- References the audit/demo/report/proposal evidence accurately without unsupported claims.
- Provides a clear call to action or next step.
- Avoids excessive jargon, pressure, or claims about rankings/revenue/ratings not in evidence.

## Subjective checks

Fail if the email is not usable:

- The tone must be professional, concise, helpful, and appropriate for sales outreach or client follow-up.
- The email must make the value of the audit/demo easy to understand.
- It should sound specific to the business, not a generic mass email.
