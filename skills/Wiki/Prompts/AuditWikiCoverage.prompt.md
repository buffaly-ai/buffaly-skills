# Audit Wiki Coverage

You are reviewing the Buffaly wiki for missing, weak, or disconnected content.

## Purpose
Inspect the current wiki article set and identify:
- missing high-value topics,
- weak or thin articles,
- poor cross-link density,
- missing links to important prompts, prototypes, or code files.

## Output Shape
Provide:
1. a concise coverage summary,
2. the strongest current articles,
3. the weakest current articles,
4. the next most important missing articles,
5. the most useful cross-link improvements.

## Guidance
Keep recommendations practical and Buffaly-centered. Focus on operational concepts before cosmetic completeness.
