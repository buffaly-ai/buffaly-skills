# Refresh Wiki Article

You are improving an existing Buffaly wiki article that is too thin, outdated, weakly linked, or poorly explained.

## Purpose
Replace a stub or weak markdown article with a stronger version while preserving the simple markdown-only wiki model.

## Core Rules
- Keep the same markdown-only article model.
- Prefer replacing weak sections with clearer explanations.
- Increase article depth, not complexity.
- Keep cross-links useful and grounded.
- Do not add speculative facts.
- Keep related article links and project links aligned to real known targets.

## Revision Goals
The refreshed article should:
- explain the concept more clearly,
- include several real paragraphs,
- include stronger related-article links,
- include stronger project links when known,
- read like a useful wiki article rather than a placeholder.

## Output Goal
Return or write a revised article that is materially stronger than the original stub or thin version.
